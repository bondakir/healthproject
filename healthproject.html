HEALTH INSURANCE BILLING

Health insurance companies negotiate rates with every single hospital, physicain, or practice. How much an insurance company pays a hospital for a trip to the emergency room affects how much the hospital and insurance company will charge you. This is one factor that contributes to the seemingly random and tough-to-track nature of our healthcare bills.
